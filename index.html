A night in the desert

Unforgettable experience

In 2023 I was lucky enough to be able to go
on an academic exchange to Madrid, where
I had the opportunity to travel to Morocco
with friends to spend a night in
the Sahara desert.

We flew to Marrakech where the 'Moroco
Sahara' agency received us and we traveled
by road to the desert where we rode camels,
drank tea, did sandbord and danced
to Moroccan music.

Agency Moroco Sahara

I recommend this incredible experience if you
are going on an exchange or if you are close
to the country. It was something different that
I had the opportunity to do with friends
and I will remember it for a lifetime.
